Personalized Health Risk Assessment Tool

Problem: Build a system that analyzes lifestyle factors, family history, and biometrics to predict future health risks and suggest preventive measures.

Skills Demonstrated:

AI/ML: Implement risk scoring models using decision trees or logistic regression for diabetes, heart disease, obesity risk

Critical Thinking: Weight different risk factors appropriately, understand population vs individual risk, consider intervention effectiveness

Problem Solving: Handle missing health data, conflicting risk factors, age-specific calculations

Modular Structure: Separate risk calculation engines for different conditions, lifestyle assessment, and recommendation modules

Clear Architecture: Flow from health inputs → risk calculation → prevention recommendations → action plan

Deliverable: Risk assessment tool that provides personalized prevention strategies with priority rankings.
